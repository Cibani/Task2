Types of Flowers

Roses Lilies Sunflowers Daisies Orchids

Roses

Roses are a classic choice for romantic occasions.

Lilies

Lilies are known for their elegant and trumpet-shaped blooms.

Sunflowers

Sunflowers are bright and cheerful, and often symbolize warmth and happiness.

Daisies

Daisies are a classic and simple flower, often associated with innocence and purity.

Orchids

Orchids are exotic and elegant, and often symbolize luxury and beauty.
